The Brand new Marvel's game called Marvel Rivals is officially on Steam.


Marvel Rivals is a third-person hero shooter video game developed and published by NetEase Games in collaboration with Marvel Games. The game was released in the 6th of December 2024 for Microsoft Window , Playstation 5 and Xbox Series X/S

Gameplay Enviroment

Marvel Rivals is a 6v6 pvp third person heroic comabt game, with a variety of Marvel based on comic characters offer freely to play! The game also include a destructible enviroment givng user an even more exciting gameplay.
As of the December of 2024 the game offers 33 playable characters both from Marvel Cinematic Universe and Marvel Comics .

Player role

Other than that there are 3 role in the game, Player could play as:
Vangaurd which is known as a frontline heroes with a agressive play style blocking the incoming damage suitable for a player who love tanky hero.
Duelist is a highly dealed damage heroes with a lower HP but high movement speed suitable for a backline attack heroes a compliment for every key players out there.
Strategist is a support type heroes with low damage and HP in exchange for a healing abilities suitable for a backline play supporting the team to victories.
